About Us

The Choco Monarch
The Choco Monarch Coffee Shop is a concept café and chocolataria emphasizing on the growing trend of café which can be easily seen in the Middle East region.

Since 2017 Choco Monarch has been at the center stage of all the lovers for delights in French Crepes, Waffles, Pancakes, freshly baked cakes and array of hot and cold beverages created in-house maintaining high standards of quality with great taste.

Strategically located in Dubai and Sharjah with its expansive menu to offer, Choco Monarch has been offering its best of contemporary and innovative delights.
